🎤 Voice Analysis

This AI-driven acoustic module evaluates vocal biomarkers for early signs of neurodegeneration. Up to 90% of Parkinson's patients develop early speech variants like Hypophonia (decreased vocal volume), vocal breathiness, or monotone speech loops. This engine evaluates fine variations in vocal frequencies to pick up on micro-tremors before they become audible to standard clinical observations.

Instructions to Use:

  1. 1 Ensure you are seated in a completely quiet room with minimal background ambient noise.
  2. 2 Click the 🎤 Record button below to open your device microphone capture stream.
  3. 3 Read the highlighted phrase displayed in the recording card aloud in a clear, natural voice.
  4. 4 Click ⏹ Stop when finished, then select 🚀 Analyse to process your acoustic matrix.
