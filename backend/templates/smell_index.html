Olfactory Smell Test Assessment

Degraded smell recognition (known as Anosmia or Hyposmia) stands as one of the primary prodromal markers of early Parkinson's Disease progression. This diagnostic test checks your scent recognition score against an ensemble medical dataset to identify subtle neurological markers up to 5 to 10 years before motor issues arise.

Instructions & Assessment Steps:

01.

Enter your baseline demographic age parameter to calibrate dataset matching structures.

02.

You will evaluate a total sequence of 20 successive multiple-choice sensory cards.

03.

Carefully analyze each smell prompt item and click the element that matches your perception.

04.

Maintain consistent pacing. Your final profile score updates automatically on your portal dashboard upon submission.
