Understanding Parkinson's Disease

What is Parkinson's?

Parkinson's disease is a progressive nervous system disorder that affects movement. Symptoms start gradually, sometimes with a barely noticeable tremor in just one hand. Early detection can significantly improve quality of life through timely intervention.
