Handwriting Assessment (Micrographia Test)

Parkinson’s disease progressively impacts fine motor movements, leading to a condition known as Micrographia—where handwriting becomes abnomally small, cramped, or crowded. This computer vision module evaluates line structures, geometric deviations, and spacing metrics from your uploaded drawing pattern to screen for subtle motor rigidity.

Instructions & Assessment Steps:

01.

Take a blank sheet of white paper and draw a steady spiral pattern or write a standard short sentence.

02.

Capture a clear image of your paper sample using your mobile phone or laptop webcam under bright lighting.

03.

Click the button below, upload the image into the workspace box, and check the live file preview layout.

04.

Hit the analyze control button. The vision framework calculates geometric deviations to process results.
